# Welcome to Owlog

Welcome to Owlog, the blogging platform for curious minds. This documentation is your guide to discovering, reading, and engaging with content on Owlog.

Whether you're here to read your favorite author, discover new ideas, or engage in discussions, we're here to help you get the most out of your experience.

## Getting started

If you're new to Owlog, we recommend starting with our **Getting started** guide. It will walk you through creating an account, signing in, and exploring the homepage.

- [Getting started overview](./getting-started/README.md)

## Exploring the documentation

This documentation is organized by tasks to help you find the information you need quickly.

- **Reading**: Learn how to discover, search for, and read articles.
- **Engagement**: Find out how to interact with authors and other readers.
- **Account**: Manage your profile, notifications, and privacy settings.
- **Help**: Get answers to frequently asked questions and contact support.

We hope you enjoy your time on Owlog!
