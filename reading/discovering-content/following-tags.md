# Following tags

Tags are specific keywords that authors add to their articles. Following tags helps you customize your feed with content that matters to you.

## How to follow a tag

1.  When you find a tag you're interested in, either on an article or on a topic page, click on it.
2.  You will be taken to the tag's page, which shows all articles with that tag.
3.  Click the **Follow** button at the top of the page.

Articles with tags you follow will appear in your "Recommended for you" feed on the homepage.
