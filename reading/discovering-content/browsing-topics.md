# Browsing topics

Topics are a great way to explore content in your areas of interest. Owlog organizes articles into various topics, making it easy to find what you're looking for.

## How to browse topics

1.  Click on the **Topics** link in the main navigation bar.
2.  You will see a list of available topics.
3.  Click on a topic to see a feed of articles related to it.
