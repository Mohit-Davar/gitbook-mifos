# Discovering articles

Owlog offers several ways to discover new and interesting articles. This guide will show you how.

## Discovering articles from the homepage

The homepage is your gateway to discovering content. You can find articles in the following sections:

*   **Featured articles**: Hand-picked articles by the Owlog team.
*   **Trending topics**: See what's popular right now.
*   **Latest articles**: A chronological feed of new content.
*   **Recommended for you**: Articles tailored to your interests.
