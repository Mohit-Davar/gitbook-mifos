# Searching for content

Owlog's search feature allows you to find articles, authors, and topics quickly.

## How to search

1.  Click the **Search** icon in the navigation bar.
2.  Enter your search query in the search bar. You can search for keywords, author names, or topic titles.
3.  Press **Enter** or click the search button.

The search results page will display a list of matching articles, authors, and topics.
