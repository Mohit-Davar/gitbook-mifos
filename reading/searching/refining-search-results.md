# Refining search results

After performing a search, you can refine the results to find exactly what you need.

## Filtering search results

You can filter search results by:

*   **Content type**: Choose between articles, authors, or topics.
*   **Date**: Filter articles by publication date (e.g., last 24 hours, last week, last month).
*   **Tags**: Narrow down results by specific tags.

## Sorting search results

You can sort search results by:

*   **Relevance**: The default sorting option, which shows the most relevant results first.
*   **Date**: Sort articles from newest to oldest, or vice versa.
*   **Popularity**: Sort articles by the number of likes or comments.
