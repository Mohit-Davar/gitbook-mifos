# Reading articles

Owlog is designed to provide a clean and comfortable reading experience.

## Reading an article

When you open an article, you'll find the content in a clean, easy-to-read format.

*   **Estimated read time**: See how long it will take to read the article.
*   **Author information**: Learn more about the author and follow them for future content.
*   **Table of contents**: For longer articles, a table of contents may be available to help you navigate.

## Interacting with the article

While reading, you can:

*   **Like** the article to show your appreciation.
*   **Comment** to share your thoughts and engage in discussion.
*   **Bookmark** the article to save it for later.
*   **Share** the article with others.
