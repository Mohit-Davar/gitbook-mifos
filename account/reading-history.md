# Reading history

Owlog keeps a history of the articles you have read, making it easy to find them again.

## How to view your reading history

1.  Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2.  Select **Reading history** from the dropdown menu.

You will see a chronological list of all the articles you have opened.

## Managing your reading history

You can remove articles from your reading history.

1.  Go to your reading history page.
2.  Find the article you want to remove.
3.  Click the **Remove** button next to the article.

**Note**: Removing an article from your reading history is permanent and cannot be undone.
