# Managing your profile

Your Owlog profile is your public identity on the platform. This guide explains how to manage it.

## How to access your profile settings

1.  Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2.  Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3.  Navigate to the **Profile** tab.

## What you can change

From the profile settings page, you can update:

*   **Profile picture**: Upload a new photo of yourself.
*   **Username**: Change your public username.
*   **Bio**: Write a short description about yourself.
*   **Social links**: Add links to your social media profiles.
