# Notification preferences

You can customize the notifications you receive from Owlog to stay informed without being overwhelmed.

## How to manage your notifications

1.  Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2.  Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3.  Navigate to the **Notifications** tab.

## Types of notifications

You can enable or disable notifications for:

*   **New followers**: When someone follows you.
*   **Comments and replies**: When someone comments on your article or replies to your comment.
*   **Likes and reactions**: When someone likes or reacts to your article.
*   **Owlog updates**: News and updates from the Owlog team.

You can choose to receive notifications via email or as push notifications in your browser.
