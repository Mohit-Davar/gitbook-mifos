# Privacy and security

This guide covers the settings available to protect your privacy and secure your account.

## How to access privacy and security settings

1.  Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2.  Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3.  Navigate to the **Privacy & Security** tab.

## Privacy settings

*   **Profile visibility**: Choose whether your profile is public or private. A private profile is only visible to people you approve.
*   **Blocked users**: Manage the list of users you have blocked.
