# Overview: Account

This section provides guidance on managing your Owlog account settings.

By the end of this guide, you will be able to:

*   Customize your public profile.
*   Manage your notification preferences.
*   Control your privacy and security settings.
*   View your reading history.

## Pages in this section

*   **Managing your profile**: Learn how to update your profile information.
*   **Notification preferences**: Control the notifications you receive from Owlog.
*   **Privacy and security**: Manage your account's privacy and security settings.
*   **Reading history**: View the articles you have read.
