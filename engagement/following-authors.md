# Following authors

Following an author is a great way to stay updated with their latest work.

## How to follow an author

1.  Navigate to an author's profile page or find one of their articles.
2.  Click the **Follow** button next to their name.

Once you follow an author, their new articles will appear in your personalized feed on the homepage.
