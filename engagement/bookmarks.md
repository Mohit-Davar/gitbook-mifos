# Bookmarks

Bookmarks allow you to save articles to read later.

## How to bookmark an article

1.  While reading an article, click the **Bookmark** icon.
2.  The article will be added to your bookmarks.

## Accessing your bookmarks

1.  Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2.  Select **Bookmarks** from the dropdown menu.

You will see a list of all the articles you have bookmarked.
