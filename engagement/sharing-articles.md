# Sharing articles

If you find an article you think others would enjoy, you can easily share it.

## How to share an article

1.  At the top or bottom of an article, look for the **Share** button.
2.  Click the button to open the sharing options.
3.  You can share the article on social media, via email, or by copying the link.

### Sharing on social media

Select the social media platform you want to share to (e.g., Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn). You will be prompted to log in to your social media account and customize the post.

### Sharing via email

Select the email option to open your default email client with a pre-populated message containing the article link.

### Copying the link

Select the copy link option to copy the article's URL to your clipboard. You can then paste it anywhere you like.
