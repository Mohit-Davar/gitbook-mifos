# Overview: Engagement

This section explains how you can interact with articles, authors, and other readers on Owlog.

By the end of this guide, you will be able to:

*   Save articles for later reading.
*   Follow your favorite authors.
*   Participate in discussions.
*   Show your appreciation for articles.
*   Share content with your network.

## Pages in this section

*   **Bookmarks**: Learn how to save articles to read later.
*   **Following authors**: Keep up with your favorite writers.
*   **Comments and discussions**: Join the conversation.
*   **Likes and reactions**: Show your appreciation for great content.
*   **Sharing articles**: Share what you're reading with others.
