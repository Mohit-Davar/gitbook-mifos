# Comments and discussions

You can share your thoughts and engage in discussions by leaving comments on articles.

## How to leave a comment

1.  Scroll to the bottom of an article.
2.  You will find the comments section.
3.  Write your comment in the text box.
4.  Click **Post comment**.

Your comment will be visible to other readers. Please be respectful and follow our community guidelines.

## Replying to comments

You can reply to other people's comments to start a thread.

1.  Find the comment you want to reply to.
2.  Click the **Reply** button below the comment.
3.  Write your reply and click **Post reply**.
