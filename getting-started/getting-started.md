# Overview: Getting started

Welcome to the getting started guide for Owlog. This section will help you create an account, sign in, and begin exploring the platform.

By the end of this guide, you will be able to:

*   Create and verify your Owlog account.
*   Sign in to the platform.
*   Navigate the homepage and discover content.

## Pages in this section

*   **Creating an account**: Learn how to create your Owlog account, verify your email address, and reset your password if you forget it.
*   **Signing in**: A step-by-step guide to signing in to your account.
*   **Exploring the homepage**: An introduction to the layout of the Owlog homepage and how to find your way around.
