# Exploring the homepage

The Owlog homepage is your starting point for discovering new content and authors. This guide provides an overview of its layout and features.

## Homepage sections

The homepage is divided into several sections to help you find what you're looking for:

*   **Featured articles**: A curated selection of the best articles on Owlog.
*   **Trending topics**: Popular topics and tags that are currently being discussed.
*   **Latest articles**: A feed of the most recently published articles.
*   **Recommended for you**: A personalized feed of articles based on your reading history and interests.

## Navigation bar

The navigation bar at the top of the page provides quick access to key areas of the platform:

*   **Home**: Returns you to the homepage.
*   **Topics**: Browse articles by category.
*   **Search**: Find articles, authors, and topics.
*   **Bookmarks**: Access your saved articles.
*   **Profile**: Manage your account and profile settings.
