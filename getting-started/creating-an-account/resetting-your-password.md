# Resetting your password

If you forget your password, you can easily reset it to regain access to your Owlog account.

## How to reset your password

1. Navigate to the Owlog sign-in page.
2. Click the **Forgot password?** link.
3. Enter the email address associated with your Owlog account and click **Send reset link**.
4. Check your email inbox for a password reset email from Owlog.
5. Open the email and click the **Reset password** button.
6. You will be prompted to enter a new password. Choose a strong password and confirm it.
7. Click **Save password**.

You can now sign in to your account with your new password.
