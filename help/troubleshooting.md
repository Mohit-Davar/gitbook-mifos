# Troubleshooting

This guide provides solutions to common technical issues you might encounter on Owlog.

## I can't sign in

*   **Check your password**: Make sure you are entering the correct password. If you have forgotten it, you can [reset your password](../getting-started/creating-an-account/resetting-your-password.md).
*   **Check your email**: Ensure you are using the email address associated with your account.

## Pages are not loading correctly

*   **Clear your browser cache**: Sometimes, old data stored in your browser can cause issues. Clearing your cache and cookies might help.
*   **Try a different browser**: If the issue persists, try accessing Owlog from a different web browser to see if the problem is browser-specific.

## I'm not receiving emails from Owlog

*   **Check your spam folder**: Emails from Owlog might sometimes end up in your spam or junk folder.
*   **Check your notification settings**: Make sure you have enabled email notifications in your [notification preferences](../account/notification-preferences.md).

If you are still experiencing issues, please [contact support](./contacting-support.md).
